Section 1: Scroll Tracking

This section helps test scroll velocity and pause detection. As you scroll through this page, the tracking SDK monitors:

  • Scroll velocity (how fast you scroll)
  • Scroll pauses (when you stop to read)
  • Scroll depth milestones (25%, 50%, 75%, 90%)
  • Time spent in different sections

The Revenue Magick platform uses this data to understand user engagement patterns and identify high-intent behaviors.

Section 2: Behavioral Analysis

Digital Body Language™ goes beyond traditional analytics by tracking micro-behaviors that reveal subconscious buying intent:

  • Hesitation Loops: Back button usage and repeated visits
  • Click Cadence: The rhythm and decisiveness of user clicks
  • Mouse Movements: Patterns that indicate engagement or confusion
  • Viewport Engagement: Which elements capture attention

Try using your browser's back button or clicking rapidly to see these behaviors in action.

Section 3: Neuromind Profiles™

Based on the behavioral data collected, users are classified into psychological profiles:

  • Fast-Mover: Quick decisions, minimal details needed
  • Proof-Driven: Wants evidence, case studies, testimonials
  • Reassurer: Needs support, help sections, guarantees
  • Skeptic: High bounce rate, limited engagement
  • Optimizer: Compares options, detailed analysis
  • Authority-Seeker: Looks for expert opinions, credentials
  • Experience-First: Values user experience, demos, trials

Your behavior on this page is being analyzed to determine your profile!
